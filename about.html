About DentaOferta

Helping patients compare dental options before they go.

DentaOferta helps patients submit guided photos and request preliminary price estimates from nearby dental clinics before scheduling a visit.

What we are building

DentaOferta is a local dental lead and price-estimate platform designed to make the first step of dental care clearer for patients and more efficient for clinics.

Patients can share photos, ZIP code, and a short description of their dental issue. Clinics can review the case and send a preliminary estimate before the patient books an appointment.

Why it matters

Many patients delay dental care because they do not know what the visit may cost. DentaOferta helps reduce uncertainty by allowing patients to compare preliminary estimates from local clinics.

  • Patients get more clarity before scheduling.
  • Clinics receive better-qualified patient cases.
  • Final diagnosis and pricing always stay with licensed dental professionals.

Important medical disclaimer

DentaOferta does not provide diagnosis, treatment, prescriptions, or medical advice. Any estimate is preliminary and must be confirmed by a licensed dental clinic after an in-person exam, X-rays, or other required tests.

If you have facial swelling, fever, heavy bleeding, difficulty breathing, or severe pain, seek urgent medical care immediately.
