Final pricing and treatment

Final price, diagnosis, and treatment decisions must be confirmed by a licensed dental clinic after an in-person exam, X-rays, or other required tests.

Clinic estimates

Any estimate provided by a clinic is preliminary and may change after the patient is evaluated in person. DentaOferta does not guarantee price, availability, quality of care, or treatment outcome.

Emergency care

If you have facial swelling, fever, heavy bleeding, difficulty breathing, or severe pain, do not wait for an online estimate. Seek urgent medical or dental care immediately.
