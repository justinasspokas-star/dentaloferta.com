Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

This page explains how DentaOferta may collect and use information submitted by patients and clinics.

Information we may collect

DentaOferta may collect information you submit through the website, including name, email, phone number, ZIP code, dental concern description, clinic information, and uploaded photos.

How information is used

Patient information is used to help request preliminary dental price estimates from local clinics. Clinic information is used to review partnership requests and founding clinic access.

Sharing with clinics

When a patient submits a case, relevant case information may be shared with nearby participating dental clinics for the purpose of providing a preliminary estimate. DentaOferta does not provide diagnosis or treatment.

Data deletion

You may request deletion of your submitted information by contacting us at justinasspokas@gmail.com.

Medical information

Do not use DentaOferta for emergencies. If you have facial swelling, fever, heavy bleeding, difficulty breathing, or severe pain, seek urgent medical care immediately.
